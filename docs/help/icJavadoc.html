InterClient Reference Member Tags

JDBC 1 | JDBC 2 | JDBC 2 Standard Extension | InterClient Extension
The API from which this member derives.
not supported
An unsupported member.
proposed for future release | proposed for InterClient 2.x
A member which has been given some preliminary thought, and we're proposing it for some future release. If it's an InterClient Extension, then the member signature is being proposed for review, just as JavaSoft puts its interfaces out for review. The member signature may change due to reviewer comment up until it is tagged since InterClient 2.x. After being tagged with since, a member can only be removed after being deprecated for at least one release.
since InterClient 1.x
The member is released. The member may only be removed after being deprecated for at least one release.
behavior subject to further functional refinements
A member is released but it may not be completely implemented, or its behavior could change in a future release. For example, see ResultSetMetaData.isReadOnly(), ResultSetMetaData.isWritable, and ResultSetMetaData.isDefinitelyWritable().
with extended behavior in JDBC 2
A JDBC 1 method whose behavior has been modified or extended in JDBC 2.
