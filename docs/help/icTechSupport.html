InterClient Technical Support

InterBase customers with purchased product support may call our 800-number Customer Support Hotline. Visit our Web site www.interbase.com or call 831-431-6500 for information about customer services available.

InterBase and InterClient information is also available via a newsgroup monitored by InterBase. Set your news reader to forums.inprise.com/interbase.public.general. This is a forum to discuss your experience with InterBase or InterClient, submit feature requests, and ask technical questions about InterBase products.

Bug reports may be submitted to interclient@interbase.com. Customers without support contracts are welcome to send questions to interclient@interbase.com but we may not be able to respond to all questions due to the volume of mail.
